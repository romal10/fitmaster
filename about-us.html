About Us

What we have done

Welcome to Daud Oyama Kyokushin Karate Club, proudly located in Jalalabad city. Led by our esteemed 5th Dan Master, Daud Oyama, a champion in karate with years of experience in national and international tournaments, we are dedicated to teaching the true spirit of Kyokushin Kai Karate. Our club focuses on discipline, strength, and personal development through rigorous training, fostering respect and perseverance. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced student, our club offers a supportive environment to help you reach your full potential. Join us on the path to mastery!
